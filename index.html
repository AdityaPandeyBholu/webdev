Wi-fi

Wi-Fi is a wireless technology that lets devices connect to the internet without cables.

Sometimes people try to access someone else’s Wi-Fi network without permission, which is called Wi-Fi hacking.

Doing this without authorization is illegal and against cyber security rules.

Security experts study Wi-Fi hacking methods to understand network weaknesses.

They use this knowledge to protect networks and improve security.

This process is known as ethical hacking.

Strong passwords and modern encryption help keep Wi-Fi networks safe.

Learning about Wi-Fi security helps people protect their internet and personal data.
